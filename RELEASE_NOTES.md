# 🏥 CareFusion Hospital Management System - v1.0.0

## 📝 Project Overview
**CareFusion** is a next-generation, comprehensive Hospital Management System (HMS) designed to streamline medical workflows, enhance patient care, and automate administrative tasks. It provides a unified platform for doctors, patients, staff, and administrators to interact seamlessly in a high-density, real-time healthcare environment.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 👨‍⚕️ For Doctors
- **Advanced Consultation Suite**: Integrated prescription management and patient history tracking.
- **Smart Diagnostics**: AI-powered diagnostic test recommendations.
- **Dynamic Scheduling**: Real-time management of availability and appointment queues.

### 🤒 For Patients
- **Smart Booking System**: Simplified appointment scheduling with real-time doctor availability.
- **Family Management**: Manage multiple family profiles under a single account.
- **Digital Medical Ledger**: Access prescriptions, lab reports, and insurance claims instantly.

### 🏛️ For Hospital Staff & Admin
- **Insurance Claim Assistant**: Automated generation and tracking of medical insurance claims.
- **Laboratory & Pharmacy Hubs**: Complete lifecycle management for tests and medication dispensing.
- **Unified Dashboard**: High-level overview of hospital operations, finances, and resource allocation.

---

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
- **Frontend**: React.js with Modern "Healthcare Modern" Design System (Vanilla CSS).
- **Backend**: Node.js & Express.js (High-performance REST API).
- **Search Engine**: **Typesense** (Instant, typo-tolerant search for medicines and diagnostics).
- **Database**: Professional medical-grade data structure.
- **DevOps**: Docker & Docker Compose for seamless deployment.
